## 🧭 About the Event

Gluten is an open-source project focused on accelerating data processing. It enhances the performance of engines like Spark through native vectorized execution, decoupled backend architecture, and modern hardware optimization.

This conference will bring together technical experts from the community, industry, and academia to explore topics such as **Apache Gluten project updates, execution optimization practices, multi-backend integration, and open-source governance**.
